How to Play DumbGame

Controls

Player 1 (WASD + F/G/H):
Move: A/D
Jump: W (double-tap for double jump)
Down: S
Attack: F (+ direction for up/down attacks)
Special: G
Ultimate: H

Player 2 (Arrows + K/L/;):
Move: ←/→
Jump: ↑ (double-tap for double jump)
Down: ↓
Attack: K (+ direction for up/down attacks)
Special: L
Ultimate: ;

Pause: ESC

Special Abilities

🔥 Fireball: Shoots a fast projectile that deals damage and knockback.
🚀 Dash: Quickly dash forward, damaging opponents in your path.
🌀 Teleport: Instantly move behind your opponent for surprise attacks.
🛡️ Shield: Gain a temporary shield that blocks incoming attacks.
⚡ Lightning: Strike your opponent with a powerful bolt from anywhere on the stage.

Ultimate Abilities

☄️ Meteor Storm: Rain meteors from the sky, dealing massive area damage.
⏰ Time Freeze: Freeze your opponent in place for a short time and gain a speed boost.
🌪️ Cyclone Fury: Summon a giant tornado that pulls in and launches opponents.
🧲 Magnet Mayhem: Create a magnetic field that pulls all projectiles and players toward you, then explodes.
⚡ Lightning Blitz: Dash at super speed, hitting multiple times and becoming invulnerable.

Game Rules & Mechanics

  • Players lose a stock if their damage reaches 175% or if they fall off the stage.
  • Double jump is available for both players.
  • Ultimate meter charges by dealing and taking damage, and passively over time.
  • Special abilities have cooldowns; use them wisely!
  • Projectiles can be blocked by shields or dodged with dashes/teleports.
  • Stage hazards may appear randomly (e.g., falling rocks, wind gusts).
  • Screen shake and particle effects for impactful hits.
  • CPU opponent available with adjustable difficulty.
  • Customize your character’s stats before each match.
